Executive Summary

GenieBridge: Natural Language Data Intelligence in Teams

Bypassing technical hurdles to bring real-time, governed Databricks insights directly into the executive decision-making workflow.

Strategic Initiative 1.0

Bridging the gap between "Data Available" and "Data Actionable"

Organizations invest millions in centralized Lakehouses, yet business leaders still rely on manual data pulls or static dashboards. GenieBridge connects the semantic reasoning power of Databricks AI/BI Genie directly to the ubiquity of Microsoft Teams.

By enabling natural language queries in a native chat interface, we empower non-technical users to interrogate massive enterprise datasets instantly and securely.

The Business Case

Solving High Friction in Current Data Workflows

Identifying the disconnect between enterprise data availability and front-line business agility.

The Executive

Dashboard Fatigue

Leaders are forced to break their workflow, log into web portals, locate reports, and decode filters just to answer a standard operational question.

Impact: Delayed decisions.
The Data Team

Analyst Bottlenecks

Impact: High cost of low-value work.

When official paths are slow, business units export Lakehouse data to local Excel files or unapproved AI tools to get quick answers.

Impact: Loss of auditability.

Data Presentation Rules

Deterministic Visual Rendering Logic

The middleware does not guess. It employs a strict matrix to ensure charts are generated correctly, defaulting to tables to prevent hallucinated visualizations.

Gateway Logic

Decision Pathway

1
Schema Parse

Review columns & data types.

2
Axis Detection

Find dates or string categories.

3
Component Choice

Select Bar, Line, or Table view.

4
Safe Fallback

If ambiguous, render Table-only.

Shape to Component Mapping

Data Shape DetectedOptimal UI ChoiceBusiness Justification
Date + FloatTrend LineHighlights velocity over a defined period.
String + FloatBar ChartSupports visual comparison of categorical volume.
Multiple DimensionsData TablePrevents incorrect grouping assumptions.
1 Row + Multiple KPIsSummary TableExecutive view without an unnatural X-axis.

Example: Handling Time Series Safely

When asked for "monthly production," Genie returns Year, Month, and Volume columns. The gateway recognizes Year/Month as a single chronological axis, avoiding the trap of rendering "Month ID" as a second measure.
